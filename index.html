Current Project

FocusHut

Students can choose between two study methods: The Pomodoro Technique or The Feynman Technique. Both of the study methods will use a timer which allocates a certain amount of time to studying a particular topic. If students choose the Feynman Technique, they will be encouraged to write what they have learned into a simple and concise paragraph. Along with these two study methods, FocusHut will contain soothing graphics to provide a calm study environment. FocusHut will also provide relaxing background music to reduce the stress students may feel as they study.

So far we have implemented a tasks class which converts the tasks into a dictionary which contains their id, name, category, and completion status. We also are able to keep track of them through a JSON file which holds their status and information. The program so far is able to ask for user input by giving them four options to choose from. We have also looked into Tkinter for the GUI aspect of our project since we aren’t familiar with it. We also discussed possible ways to organize our GitHub to separate our classes from our main function.
