About Me

I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the University of Oregon, with minors in Creative Writing and Mathematics. This combination allows me to explore both the logical structure of code and the expressive power of language.

Alongside my studies, I work as a writing tutor at the university’s Tutoring and Academic Engagement Center, where I support students in developing their writing skills across disciplines. I’ve also served as a peer mentor for the Pathway Oregon Scholarship Program, guiding first-year students through their transition into college life and helping them find community on campus.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and writing poetry across a range of styles and voices. I’m especially passionate about making computer science more accessible to students from all backgrounds, regardless of their prior experience. I believe that access to education empowers younger generations to learn, grow, and shape the future.
